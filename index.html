Description of the artist's ideology:

Niall Ferguson's book, ‘Civilization’, introduces six key succession formulas in civilization: Competition, Science, Property, Medicine, Consumption, and Work. What would the next one be? I want to add Art. I believe that the future of human beings will be sustainable with the help of art. When people are asked why they exist, during the rapid development of technology, Art will be a means to articulate the reason. This understanding of art goes beyond what we usually speak of. It is the art that creates the civilization that can coexist with machines and nature.



Currently I am exploring logo and web designing pursuing CS + Advertising in the future
